About Guestcheck.io

What exactly is that and how does the guest check-in work?

We founded Guestcheck.io for one reason only. We believe in a common existence and survival. After our first dinner going on 19.05.2020 we saw how annoying it is to collect the data of every single person and then to keep it safe. The founders behind Guestcheck.io actually come from a software sector in the health care industry and so they know a lot about data protection

Together with restaurateurs, they then bundled all their energy and developed a product that is only there for the duration of the print runs. It is a full-service solution for all hosts*. Your customers scan the QR Code at the entrance or table and we do the rest. You don't have to worry about the data and its security anymore. We are there for you and in case of a report, we will transmit all the necessary information to the appropriate authority.
